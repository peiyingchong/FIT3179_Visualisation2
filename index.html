Intentional self-harm, Age-standardised death rates by Country of birth, 2018-2022

Excluding Australia, England was the most common country of birth for those who died by suicide (757 deaths).
When adjusted for population size and age-structure, those with a country of birth in England did not have the highest suicide rate of those born overseas.
Those born in Croatia, New Zealand and Scotland had a higher suicide rate than those born in Australia.
